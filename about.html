About NanoKineticsDB

Nanozymes are nanomaterials with intrinsic enzyme-like characteristics. They have emerged as highly stable and low-cost alternatives to natural enzymes, finding extensive applications in biosensing, environmental treatment, and therapeutics.

The goal of the NanoKinetics Global Database is to provide researchers with a centralized, searchable platform to compare the kinetic parameters (such as Vmax and Km) of various nanomaterials across different substrates and environmental conditions.
