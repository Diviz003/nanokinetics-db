About NanoKinetics DB

NKDB is a specialized Database Management System designed to store nanozymes with their kinetic activity alongside various substrates[cite: 1].

What is a Nanozyme?

A nanozyme is a physical nanomaterial characterized by its specific material composition, size, and shape[cite: 1]. These entities act as catalysts that interact with various chemical substrates to drive reactions[cite: 1]. The efficiency of a nanozyme is measured through its kinetic parameters—specifically, its maximum rate of reaction (Vmax) and its binding affinity to a substrate (Km) under defined experimental conditions like temperature and pH[cite: 1]. Because a single nanozyme can react with multiple different substrates, and vice versa, their experimental behavior forms complex many-to-many relationships that this database is designed to structure and standardize[cite: 1].

The Challenge & Our Solution

The Problem: Essential scientific knowledge regarding physical properties and experimental results currently remains fragmented across spreadsheets and publications with no unified access point[cite: 1].

Our Solution: A centralized, transactional SQL database that normalizes physical, chemical, and kinetic parameters within a single relational framework[cite: 1]. It is built utilizing a robust 3-Tier Architecture featuring a secure backend API and an interactive client interface[cite: 1].
